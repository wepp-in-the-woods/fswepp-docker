Soil and Water Engineering   >   Information   >   Upland Watersheds > Watershed Data

 Data presented here were collected on three Interior Northwest watersheds that were harvested and then burned using prescribed fire. The data were used to test the prediction accuracy of a modified WEPP developed for forests and a geospatial version of WEPP called GeoWEPP Arc X. The project is presented in detail in the Masters Thesis Accuracy Assessment of WEPP-based Erosion Models for Harvested and Burned Forest Watersheds.

The data used in the thesis are presented here for public use.
