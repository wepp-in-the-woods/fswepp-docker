BAER Treatment Monitoring Sites
Mixing Fire Site -- San Bernardino National Forest

The Mixing paired watershed site was installed in 1999 to measure the effectiveness of contour-felled log erosion barriers in reducing post-fire hillslope runoff and erosion. This site operated continuously until 2005, at which time it was dismantled and removed.
