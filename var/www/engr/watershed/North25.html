BAER Treatment Monitoring Sites
North 25 Site -- Wenatchee National Forest

The North 25 paired watershed site was installed in 1998 to measure the effectiveness of contour-felled log erosion barriers in reducing post-fire hillslope runoff and erosion. This site operated continuously until 2005, at which time it was dismantled and removed.
