Soil and Water Engineering  >  Information  >  Fire Effects


Mission: To develop methods of predicting the effects of natural and prescribed fire on forest hydrologic and erosion processes and to develop mitigation techniques for the effects.


Personnel:

Peter R. Robichaud, Research Engineer
William J. Elliot, Project Leader


Projects:
  • Measure and model erosion rates following wild fires on forests and rangelands.
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of erosion mitigation practices following wild fire.
