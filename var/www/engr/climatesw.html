Rock:Clime on the WWW
Create and download a WEPP climate file to your PC. Rock:Clime is an interface to the ARS Climate Generator CLIGEN. It has an expanded and improved database from the one that was distributed with CLIGEN prior to 2000. It will also access the PRISM precipitation database which has monthly precipitation amounts interpolated to a 2.5-km grid covering the entire continental U.S.
