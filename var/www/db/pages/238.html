Other Propagation Information:
1-3 weeks cold moist stratification at 3oC, then germinate at 20-30oC with light (Chirco & Turnoer 1986).
No treatment needed for fresh seed. Seed which has been stored needs cold moist stratification (Mirov 1939).
Best growth was obtained in a greenhouse with alternating day/night temperatures of at least 20/10oC (McDonough 1969).
Without pretreatment very low germination occurred at 20/30oC with alternating light. No germination occurred at any other light and temperature regimes without pretreatment. 3 weeks cold moist stratification, then alternating 20/30oC temperatures with alternating light, or 15oC in the dark resulted in 72% germ (Maguire and Overland 1959).
Reproduces sexually by seed and vegetatively by rhizomes.
