A. eatonii is a valuable forage for Rocky Mountain elk in summer and fall (Kufeld 1973). Aster species are hosts for the larva of the pearl crescent (Phyciodes tharos ssp. pascoensis) and the northern checkerspot (Charidryas palla) butterflies (Pocewicz 2005).
