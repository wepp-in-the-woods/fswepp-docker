Characteristics:
Two varieties are commonly recognized, var. intertexta with long corollas (7-10 mm) exceeding the calyx and long filaments (1.5-3.5 mm), and var. propinqua with shorter corollas (4-7 mm) and shorter filaments (0.5-2 mm long). They are sometimes treated at the subspecies level.
