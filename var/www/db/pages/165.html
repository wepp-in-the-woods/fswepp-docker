Flowers: Equisetum species do not have flowers in the traditional sense. Sexual reproduction is by spores which germinate to form a small gametophyte generation only a few mm across. The gametophytes are unisexual and fertilization requires a film of water between the male and female gametophytes. The sporophyte generation arises from the fertilized archegonium in the female gametophyte.
