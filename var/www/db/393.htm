Other Propagation Information:
Preliminary data from the Pullman Plant Materials Center indicates optimum germination occurs after 45 days of cold, moist stratification. Longer stratification does not increase germination rates. Some germination will occur without stratification.
Germinates at a constant temperature 15oC or alternating temperatures of 20/30oC in light or dark (Chirco & Turnoer 1986).
120 days of cold moist stratification with light resulted in highest germination. Germinates at low temps during stratification (Nauman 2002).
No pretreatment required (Link 1993).
Germination was only 4-8% under several light and temperature combinations without cold moist stratification (Maguire & Overland 1959).
Reproduces sexually by seed.
