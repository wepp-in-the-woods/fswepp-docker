Details of Rule of Thumb by Kuyumjian

Experienced BAER team members often use their own rule of thumb, which was developed based on their experience and post-fire monitoring/observation, and works well within certain regions. An experienced BAER hydrologist (pers. comm., Kuyumjian 2007) suggested using the following rule of thumb, which requires a minimal amount of input information.

 

Rule of Thumb

 

  Qp = 300 x Asx I x 1.25
     
    where
  Qp = peak flow in cfs;
  I = precipitation intensity in inch/hour;
  As = size of high burn severity area in mi2; and,
  1.25 = bulking factor.
