WEPP:Road road geometry


Road length: Horizontal length of road segment between successive drainage locations.
Road gradient: Decimal percent slope of the water flow path along the road surface.
Road width: Depends upon road design.
  • For insloped roads, enter ditch width + travelway width
  • For outsloped, unrutted roads, enter travelway width
  • For outsloped, rutted roads, enter rut spacing + rut width
