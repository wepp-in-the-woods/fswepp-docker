Summit Mt. Sustainability Footprint Reduction Contest
April 20 – May 29, 2015

The Rocky Mountain Research Station Sustainable Operations Green Team created this contest to help encourage the adoption of 'green' activities in your day to day work.

We challenge you to step up and participate in the Summit Mt. Sustainability Footprint Reduction Contest. Participants will compete as individuals and as part of a team based primarily on location.
  • Participants will work toward one or two 2-hour time-off awards.
  • Two group locations will be awarded $250 each for Sustainable Operations improvements of their choice.
So, go out and encourage your co-workers to participate. We all win by learning how to be more sustainable and reduce our environmental footprint!

To participate, enter your Forest Service email address (or a reasonable facsimile, only letters and numbers allowed) and your duty location, check out the actions, and then return weekly to record the 'green' activities you have completed. Some activities are weekly, some daily, and some are one-time within the contest period.
You can visit this web page and check on or correct the recording of your accomplishments, and see how your actions and your location's actions compare to others', at any time during the contest.

Actions for this contest must be recorded by noon Mountain Time June 5.

Contest Rules

  • All Rocky Mountain Research Station (RMRS) employees (full-time, part-time, temporary, etc.) and RMRS-hosted employees (students, contrators, etc.) are eligible to participate. However, only RMRS employees are eligible for Time-Off awards.
  • The contest will run for 6 weeks, from April 20 through May 29, 2015.
    • Weekly activities may be reported any time during the contest. However, only the points reported by 10 a.m. Mountain Time on the Monday following each week will be reported in the official weekly totals.
    • All activities must be reported by noon Mountain Time June 5
  • Participation will be tracked and awarded based on individuals and groups.
    • Individual participants may work toward two separate reward levels.
      • A two-hour time-off award for earning 9,000 or more points at the end of the contest.
      • An additional two-hour time-off award for earning 15,000 or more points at the end of the contest.
    • Group participation is based primarily on your location. Participants are required to report a minimum of 500 points a week for at least 3 weeks to be considered a group participant. One group cannot receive both awards. Award funds must be used for Sustainable Operations improvements. This may include education, renovation, or other projects which promote and encourage sustainable operations growth within RMRS.
      • $250 award for the highest average of points earned for smaller groups (five to 20 possible participants)
      • $250 award for the highest average of points earned for larger groups (more than 20 possible participants)
  • This contest is based on the honor system – so be truthful and record your activities carefully.
  • Every two weeks, we will post progress at each lab location.
  • You can check progress by visiting the RMRS Green Team website as well.
