Standard Forest Treatments (Organic Matter and Compaction)

  1. C0OM0 - No compaction; low organic removal: trees harvested and bole removed, but slash is left (slash = tops of trees with trunks less than 6 inches in diameter and branches).
  2. C1OM0 - Medium compaction; low organic removal: trees harvested and bole removed, but slash is left.
  3. C2OM0 - Heavy compaction; low organic removal: trees harvested and bole removed, but slash is left.
  4. C0OM1 - No compaction; medium organic removal: trees harvested and bole and slash is removed.
  5. C1OM1 - Medium compaction; medium organic removal: trees harvested and bole and slash is removed.
  6. C2OM1 - Heavy compaction; medium organic removal: trees harvested and bole and slash is removed.
  7. C0OM2 - No compaction; heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal.
  8. C1OM2 - Medium compaction; heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal.
  9. C2OM2 - Heavy compaction; heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal.

Optional Extra Forest Treatments (used on selected sites)

  1. C0/Stumped - Low organic removal: trees harvested, bole and stump removed (stump removal is an attempt to prevent the disease, Armillaria), but slash is left (slash = tops of trees with trunks less than 6 inches in diameter and branches); no compaction.
  2. Herbicide - heribicide is applied to the understory vegetation on part of a plot and not the rest (the growth rates of trees in both sections are compared - trees on part where herbicide is used will not have to compete as much with other vegetation).
  3. Winter logging - Logging done in winter in order to compare the effects to those of summer logging (Kamloops, British Columbia, Canada).
