Long Description of Figure 1:
Flow chart shows at the top: Ecosystem Resource Classes as the classification; below that there are three overall societal values: Productivity, Biological Integrity, and Aesthetics; the previous three values lead into Assessment Questions and then, Indicators; the seven ecosystem processes that are indicators are: Soil Development, Hydrologic Cycles, Atmospheric Interactions, Carbon Allocations, Biogeochemical Cycling, Desertification, and Landscape Processes.
