Long Description of Figure 2:
Flow chart showing links between key processes in Rangelands.

Socio economic affects: landscape;

Landscape affects: socio economic, terrestrial vegetation, terrestrial fauna;

Stressors affect: landscape and terrestrial fauna;

Atmospheric inputs affect: stressors, landscape, terrestrial vegetation, soil micro and macro flora/fauna, biogeochemistry, and water;

Terrestrial vegetation affects: landscape, biogeochemistry, soil micro and macro flora/fauna and atmospheric inputs;

Terrestrial fauna affects: socio economic, biogeochemistry, and landscape;

Soil micro and macro flora/fauna affects: terrestrial vegetation and biogeochemistry;

Physical Soils affect: biogeochemistry;

Water affects: terrestrial fauna, landscape, soil micro and macro flora/fauna, physical soils and biogeochemistry;

Biogeochemistry affects: soil micro and macro flora/fauna, terrestrial vegetation, and physical soils.
