2BC 40-60 ins. - Brownish yellow (10YR 6/8) extremely gravelly loam, yellow (10YR 7/8) dry; massive structure; extremely firm, sticky and plastic; common medium, few fine roots; 80 percent gravel, 15 percent cobble; very stongly acid.
