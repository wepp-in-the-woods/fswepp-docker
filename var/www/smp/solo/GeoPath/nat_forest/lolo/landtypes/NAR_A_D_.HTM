2E/B 24-27 ins. - Olive brown (2.5Y 4.5/4) extremely gravelly sandy loam, pale yellow (2.5Y 8/4) dry; single grain structure; nonsticky and nonplastic; common to many fine and medium roots; 85 percent gravel; slightly acid; abrupt wavy to discontinuous boundary.

2Bw 27-32 ins. - Olive brown (2.5Y 4/4) extremely gravelly loam, pale yellow (2.5Y 8/4) dry; weak fine and medium subangular blocky structure; nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine and medium roots; 55 percent gravel, 10 percent cobble; slightly acid.
