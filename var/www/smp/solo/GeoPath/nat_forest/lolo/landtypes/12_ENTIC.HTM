S O I L D E S C R I P T I O N #12

ENTIC CRYANDEPTS

MEDIAL/LOAMY SKELETAL, MIXED

REFERENCE PEDON. Colors are for moist soil unless otherwise indicated.

A 0-2 ins. - Dark brown (7.5YR 4/3) cobbly silt loam, light brown (7.5YR 6/4)

dry; weak fine platy structure; nonsticky and nonplastic; many fine,

medium, and coarse roots; 15 percent cobble.

Bo1 2-9 ins. - Dark brown (7.5YR 4/4) silt loam, light brown (7.5YR 6/4) dry;

moderate medium subangular blocky structure; nonsticky and nonplastic; many

very fine, fine roots.

Bo2 9-15 ins. - Dark brown (7.5YR 4/4) gravelly silt loam, light brown (10YR

6/4) dry; weak fine subangular blocky structure; nonsticky and nonplastic;

many very fine, fine roots; 30 percent gravel.

2E 15-18 ins. - Light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) extremely cobbly fine

sandy loam, light gray (10YR 7/2) dry; single grain structure;

nonsticky and nonplastic; few fine roots; 35 percent gravel, 40

percent cobble.

2Bw 18-35 ins. - Light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) extremely cobbly silt

loam, very pale brown (10YR 7/3) dry; moderate fine subangular blocky

structure; slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine, medium

roots; 20 percent gravel, 45 percent cobble.

2C 35 ins. - fractured Siltite

LOCATION AND SETTING: West-central Montana; Coeur D'Alene Mountains; Cherry

Creek drainage; SE1/4 of NE1/4, section 33, T20N, R28W; map unit 43QA. This

soil is formed in volcanic ash influenced loess overlying material derived from

metasedimentary bedrock. The slope gradient is 15 percent on a west aspect at

5,560 feet of elevation. The habitat type is subalpine fir/beargrass-grouse

whortleberry.

The Bo horizons have silt loam textures, 6 to 10 percent clay, and 5 to 25

percent coarse fragments. This horizon has other moist colors such as 10YR 5/5

and 10YR 4/4. Some soils have a shallow E horizon.

The 2E horizon has a loam to sandy loam texture, 8 to 14 percent clay, and 35

to 75 percent coarse fragments. This horizon has other moist colors such as

10YR 5/4 and 10YR 6/3. Some soils have 2E/B or 2B/E horizons underlying the 2E

horizon.

The 2Bw horizons have silt loam to sandy loam textures, 8 to 22 percent clay,

and 35 to 75 percent coarse fragments. These horizons have other moist colors

such as 10YR 5/6 and 2.5Y 4/4.
