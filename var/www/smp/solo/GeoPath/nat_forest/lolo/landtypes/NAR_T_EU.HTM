Pedon Description

O 2-0 ins. - Forest litter

Bt2 12-20 ins. - Weak red (2.5YR 4/2) clay, pale red (2.5YR 6/2) dry; medium subangular blocky structure; hard, firm, sticky and plastic; plentiful roots, few fine pores; gradual boundary.

Bt3 20-28 ins. - Weak red (2.5YR 4/2) clay, pale red (2.5YR 6/2) dry; medium subangular blocky structure; hard, firm, sticky and plastic; plentiful roots; few fine pores; clear wavy boundary.

BC 28-40 ins. - Reddish brown (5YR 5/3) clay and silty clay loam (varved), pinkish gray (5YR 6/2) dry; medium subangular blocky structure; hard, firm, sticky and plastic; few roots; very few finepores; clear wavy boundary.

C 40-64 ins. - Pinkish white (5YR 8/2) clay and silty clay loam (varved), pale red (2.5YR 6/2) dry; medium subangular blocky structure; very few roots; very few fine pores.
