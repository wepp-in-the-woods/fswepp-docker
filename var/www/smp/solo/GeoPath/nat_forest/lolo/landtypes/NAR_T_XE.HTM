Pedon Description

A 0-4 ins. - Very dark gray (10YR 3/1) gravelly sandy loam, light gray (10YR 7/2) dry; moderate fine subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine, few medium roots; 15 percent gravel; neutral; abrupt smooth boundary.

C1 4-8 ins. - Brown (10YR 5/3) gravelly sandy loam, very pale brown (10YR 7/3) dry; weak fine subangular blocky structure; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; few fine roots; 15 percent gravel; neutral, clear smooth boundary.

C2 8-20 ins. - Yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) extremely gravelly loamy sand, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry; single grain structure; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; many coarse, common fine roots; 65 percent gravel; slightly acid; clear smooth boundary.

C3 20-30 ins. - Variably colored sand grains, extremely gravelly sand; single grain structure; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine roots; 65 percent gravel; slightly acid; clear smooth boundary.

C4 30-44 ins. - Variably colored sand grains, extremely gravelly sand; single grain structure; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; 65 percent gravel; slightly acid.
