Pedon Description

E 0-2 ins. - Grayish brown (10YR 5/2) silt loam, light gray (10YR 7/2) dry; weak fine subangular blocky structure; very friable, nonsticky and slightly plastic; many coarse and medium, common fine roots; 10 percent gravel; moderately acid; abrupt irregular boundary.

E/B 2-10 ins. - Brown (10YR 5/3) and dark brown (10YR 4/3) gravelly silt loam, light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) and light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry; weak fine subangular blocky structure; friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; many medium roots; 15 percent gravel; slightly acid; abrupt wavy boundary.

C1 10-20 ins. - Pale brown (10YR 6/3) gravelly sandy loam, very pale brown (10YR 7/3) dry; weak fine subangular blocky structure; friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few medium roots; 25 percent gravel; neutral; abrupt wavy boundary.

C2 20-26 ins. - Pale brown (10YR 6/3) extremely gravelly sandy loam, very pale brown (10YR 7/3) dry; few medium roots; 95 percent gravel; neutral.

R 26 ins. - Schist bedrock
