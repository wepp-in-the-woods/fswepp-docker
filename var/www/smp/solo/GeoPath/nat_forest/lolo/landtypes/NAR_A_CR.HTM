Pedon Description

A1 0-6 ins. - Very dark gray (7.5YR 3/1) silt loam, dark brown (10YR 3/3) dry; moderate medium subangular blocky and granular structure; soft, nonsticky and nonplastic; many fine and coarse, few medium roots; 5 percent gravel; moderately acid; clear smooth boundary.

A2 6-14 ins. - Dark brown (7.5YR 3/2) silt loam, yellowish brown (10YR 5/5) dry; moderate fine and medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, nonsticky and slightly plastic; common medium and fine, few coarse roots; 10 percent gravel; slightly acid; abrupt broken boundary.

B/A 14-32 ins. - Dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) very gravelly silt loam, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry; moderate medium to strong fine subangular blocky structure; hard, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few medium, fine roots; 15 percent gravel, 20 percent cobble; neutral; clear smooth boundary.

2C 32-48 ins. - Yellowish brown (10YR 5/6) very gravelly silt loam, yellow (10YR 7/6) dry; massive structure; hard, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; 25 percent gravel, 10 percent cobble; neutral.
