A2 4-9 ins. - Very dark brown (7.5YR 2/2) stony loam, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry; strong very fine granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; abundant roots; 20 percent coarse fragments; slightly acid; gradual boundary.

A3 9-16 ins. - Very dark brown (7.5YR 2/2) stony loam, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry; strong very fine granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; abundant roots; 20 percent coarse fragments; moderately acid; gradual boundary.

A4 16-23 ins. - Very dark brown (7.5YR 2/2) stony loam, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry; strong very fine granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; many roots; 30 percent coarse fragments; moderately acid; clear wavy boundary.

Bo1 23-31 ins. - Dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) extremely stony loam, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry; weak very fine granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; many fine roots; 70 percent coarse fragments; neutral; gradual wavy boundary.

Bo2 31-38 ins. - Dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) extremely stony loam, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry; weak very fine granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; common roots; 70 percent coarse fragments; strongly acid; clear wavy boundary.

2C1 38-50 ins. - Olive brown (2.5Y 4/3) extremely stony loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few roots; 70 percent coarse fragments; strongly acid; gradual boundary.

2C2 50-72 ins. - Olive brown (2.5Y 4/3) extremely stony loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few roots; 70 percent coarse fragments; strongly acid.
