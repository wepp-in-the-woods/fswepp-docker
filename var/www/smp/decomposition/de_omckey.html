Abbreviation of Treatment Description of Treatment
UnC or Uncut

Uncut - forest left undisturbed as a control group
OM0

Low organic removal: trees harvested and bole (stump) removed, but slash is left (slash = tops of trees with trunks less than 6 inches in diameter and branches)
OM1

Medium organic removal: total tree removal; forest floor left (this treatment is not being used in the Decomposition Study)
OM2

Heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal
C0

No compaction
C1

Medium compaction (this treatment is not being used in the Decomposition Study)
C2

Heavy compaction
