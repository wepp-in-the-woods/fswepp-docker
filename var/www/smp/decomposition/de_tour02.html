Forest Treatments (Organic Matter and Compaction)

  1. UnC - Uncut - forest left undisturbed as control group.
  2. OM0C0 - Low organic removal: trees harvested and bole removed, but slash is left (slash = tops of trees with trunks less than 6 inches in diameter and branches); no compaction.
  3. OM0C2 - Low organic removal: trees harvested and bole removed, but slash is left (slash = tops of trees with trunks less than 6 inches in diameter and branches); heavy compaction.
  4. OM2C0 - Heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal; no compaction.
  5. OM2C2 - Heavy organic removal: total tree and forest floor removal; heavy compaction.

The treatments at the Priest River Experimental Forest were installed in October 1997 and May 1998.
