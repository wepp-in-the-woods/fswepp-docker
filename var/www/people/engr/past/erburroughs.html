  Edward R. Burroughs, Jr.


Ed was a Research Engineer with the USDA Forest Service at the Intermountain Research Station in Moscow, Idaho. He recognized that WEPP would be useful for forest conditions. He initiated Forest Service Research activities that led to the cooperation of the USDA Forest Service with the Agricultural Research Service in the creation of forest related WEPP components. Ed regrettably passed away in November, 1991 before he could see the completion of his efforts.
