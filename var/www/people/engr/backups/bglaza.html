  Brandon Glaza
  Civil Engineer
  USDA Forest Service
  Rocky Mountain Research Station
  1221 South Main Street
  Moscow, ID 83843

  

Brandon is a civil engineer with the USDA Forest Service, Rocky Mountain Research Station under the leadership of Bill Elliot. His main focus is to instrument and monitor a series of watershed-scale studies to determine the effects fuel treatments such as mechanical thinnings and prescribed fires have on soil erosion and sedimentation. This year's study was implemented at the Priest River Experimental Forest and next year's is planned for the Boise Basin Experimental Forest.

Brandon graduated in 2002 with a B.S. in Biosystems Engineering from Michigan State University.

Member of the American Society of Agricultural Engineers
