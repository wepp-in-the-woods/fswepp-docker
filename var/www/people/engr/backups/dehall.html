Projects:

David has been working primarily in developing slope stability simulation computer models. He has been involved in programming all three levels of analysis:

  • Level I--LISA (Level I Stability Analysis) and DLISA,
  • Level II--SARA (Stability Analysis for Road Access) and DSARA, and
  • Level III--STABL3FW (the technology of which went into XSTABL)

He is now creating customized interfaces to the WEPP (soil erosion and sediment transport model) program and to databases created by it. These interfaces (some still under development) include:

  • FS WEPP (Forest Service interfaces for WEPP, including X-DRAIN, WEPP:Road, Disturbed WEPP, and Rock:Clime)
  • Cross-Drain Spacing/Sediment Yield calculator (X-DRAIN and XDS)
  • WEPP Forest Files
