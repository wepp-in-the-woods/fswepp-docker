  Dennis E. Ferguson
  Project Leader, Forest Ecosystem Processes
  Rocky Mountain Research Station
  1221 South Main Street
  Moscow, ID 83843

  


Dennis Ferguson is a research silviculturist who has worked on these research topics: release of advance regeneration, regeneration establishment modeling, Grand Fir Mosaic reforestation problems, pocket gophers, allelopathy, habitat types, western spruce budworm defoliation, restoration of whitebark pine and western white pine ecosystems, and forest health.
